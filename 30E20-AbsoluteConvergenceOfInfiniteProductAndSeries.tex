\textbf{Theorem.}\; The infinite product 
$$\prod_{n=1}^\infty(1\!+\!c_n)$$
\PMlinkname{converges absolutely}{AbsoluteConvergenceOfInfiniteProduct} if and only if the series
$$\sum_{n=1}^\infty c_n$$
with complex \PMlinkescapetext{terms} $c_n$ \PMlinkname{converges absolutely}{AbsoluteConvergence}.\\

{\em Proof.}\; The theorem follows directly from the theorems of the entries absolutely convergent infinite product converges and \PMlinkname{infinite product of sums $1\!+\!a_i$}{InfiniteProductOfSums1a_i}.
