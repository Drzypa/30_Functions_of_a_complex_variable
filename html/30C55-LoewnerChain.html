Loewner chain


A Loewner chain is a continuous function f:𝔻×[0,∞)→ℂ (where 𝔻 refers to the open unit disc) with the following properties:

  1. 1.

    the function z↦f⁢(z,t) is analytic and univalent for all t∈[0,∞)

  2. 2.

    f⁢(0,t)=0 and f′⁢(0,t)=et

  3. 3.

    f⁢(𝔻,s)⊆f⁢(𝔻,t) for 0≤s<t<∞
