extended boundary


Definition.

Suppose that ℂ is the complex plane and G⊂ℂ. The extended boundary of G denoted ∂∞⁡G is defined as the boundary of G plus the point at infinity if in fact G is unbounded.

The extended boundary of G is really the boundary of G in the extended complex plane (the one-point compactification of ℂ).
