Proof of Möbius transformation cross-ratio preservation theorem


From the definition of Möbius transform we get that the image wk of a point zk is

wk=a⁢zk+bc⁢zk+d

From this we get

wi-wj=a⁢zi+bc⁢zi+d-a⁢zj+bc⁢zj+d=(a⁢d-b⁢c)⁢(zi-zj)(c⁢zi+d)⁢(c⁢zj+d)

and by inserting this into the cross-ratios

(w1-w2)⁢(w3-w4)(w1-w4)⁢(w3-w2)=(a⁢d-b⁢c)⁢(z1-z2)(c⁢z1+d)⁢(c⁢z2+d)⁢(a⁢d-b⁢c)⁢(z3-z4)(c⁢z3+d)⁢(c⁢z4+d)(a⁢d-b⁢c)⁢(z1-z4)(c⁢z1+d)⁢(c⁢z4+d)⁢(a⁢d-b⁢c)⁢(z3-z2)(c⁢z3+d)⁢(c⁢z2+d)=(z1-z2)⁢(z3-z4)(z1-z4)⁢(z3-z2)
