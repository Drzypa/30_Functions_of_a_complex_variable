The winding number is an analytic way to define an explicit isomorphism
 
 $$ W[\bullet:z_0]\co \pi_1(\BC\setminus z_0)\to\BZ$$
from the fundamental group of the punctured (at $z_0$) complex plane to the group 
of integers.
